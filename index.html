American History

What is America?

America is the idea of independence, justice, liberty, freedom, and opportunity. It is the pursuit of creating a more perfect society and country. These ideas can be traced back to America's origins, as European Settlers fought for freedom from British Rule. America's story is one of much struggle and persistance against hardships like this. However, these hardships are responsible for making America the diverse and vibrant nation that it is today.
